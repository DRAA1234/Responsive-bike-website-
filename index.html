Yamaha MT

The Yamaha MT 15 V2 is powered by 155cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 18.1 bhp and a torque of 14.1 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Yamaha MT 15 V2 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This MT 15 V2 bike weighs 141 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 10 liters.

Yamaha R15

The Yamaha R15 V4 is powered by a 155 cc air-cooled engine which produces 18.4 PS @ 10000 rpm of power. It has a fuel tank of 11 L and a claimed mileage of 45 kmpl. The Yamaha R15 V4 starts at Rs 1.84 and goes up to Rs 2.11 Lakh (ex-showroom, Delhi). It is available in six variants

About Us

Albert Bike Store Room is a haven for cycling enthusiasts, offering a diverse range of bicycles, accessories, and spare parts to meet every rider's needs. Located in a bustling part of town, Albert's store provides easy access and ample space for browsing. The store room is meticulously organized with high-quality shelving and display units, ensuring that customers can easily find what they are looking for. From sleek road bikes and rugged mountain bikes to comfortable hybrids and stylish cruisers, Albert's selection caters to all types of cyclists. Additionally, the store features a dedicated workshop area where skilled technicians provide expert repairs and maintenance services, ensuring that every bike is in top-notch condition.

Customer satisfaction is at the heart of Albert's Bike Store Room. The knowledgeable and friendly staff are always ready to assist customers with personalized recommendations and advice. To foster a sense of community, Albert's regularly hosts events such as bike maintenance workshops, group rides, and cycling safety seminars. The store's strong online presence, with an easy-to-navigate website and active social media engagement, keeps customers informed about the latest products and special offers. By combining a wide range of high-quality products with exceptional service, Albert's Bike Store Room has established itself as a trusted destination for cyclists in the area.
